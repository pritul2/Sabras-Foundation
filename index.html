2012 saw the beginnings of Sabras Foundation with Volansys launching "Helping Hands"

Our foundation helps uplift communities across India, giving people a second chance to transform their lives. Through impactful projects in education, health and community development, we empower thousands to build a better future. Our initiatives provide marginalized sections new opportunities for growth, igniting hope and positivity. Together, we can create an inclusive society where no one is left behind.

Our Charity Organization Activities

Kharaghoda, a small desert village in Gujarat, was the first location where Sabras Foundation started its community development work in 2019. Inhabited by marginalized salt worker families called Agariyas, this remote area with lack of basic amenities was the foundation's starting point. By providing clean water access and other impactful interventions for the Agariya community, Sabras' upliftment efforts in Kharaghoda marked the genesis of its mission to transform underserved communities across India.
