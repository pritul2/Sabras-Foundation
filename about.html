Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift and empower vulnerable communities across India through impactful interventions and compassionate service. We believe progress lies in participatory development - working together with the community, understanding their needs and challenges, and helping them become self-reliant.

We start by listening and learning from the community we seek to serve. Our on-ground efforts tackle issues of poverty, healthcare, education, nutrition, infrastructure and more. By understanding the systemic and root causes, we can drive long-term sustainable development. We aim to ignite lasting positive change.

Our Ministries

Sabras Foundation pursues integrated community development across India through its diverse initiatives in

  • Education
  • Healthcare & Nutrition
  • Community Development
  • Empowerment & Livelihoods

Our Volunteers

form the backbone of our on-ground efforts - providing teaching assistance in schools, helping run health camps, distributing supplies to communities in need. Their selfless service and commitment to the cause inspires us every day. From helping renovate Anganwadis to sponsoring education for underprivileged youth, volunteers enable us to expand our impact. Their generosity with time, skills and compassion has been invaluable towards uplifting thousands of lives. We are deeply grateful to all our volunteers who work tirelessly to create grassroots change. It is their solidarity that gives wings to our mission of empowerment.
